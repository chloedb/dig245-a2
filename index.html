How to view an HTML source in Google Chrome (Windows)

Cracking open the geode

1. Go to a desired page
2. Right click on the mouse (double finger tap on Mac)
3. Click inspect on the menu that pops up
4. A menu will open on the right side of the screen
5. Make sure you're on the elements tab in the menu
6. Go over the pieces of the code to highlight parts of the page
